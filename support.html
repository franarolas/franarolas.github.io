Support

Thanks for using one of my apps! If something isn't working, if you have feedback, or if you'd just like to say hi, I'd love to hear from you.

Contact

Email: franarolas@gmail.com

I read every message personally and try to reply within a couple of days.

Reporting a bug

When reporting a bug, it helps a lot if you include:

• Which app and version you're using
• Your device model and operating system version
• A description of what you expected to happen and what actually happened
• Steps to reproduce the issue if you can

Feature requests

I'm always happy to hear ideas for new features or content. No promises, but the best ideas often come from players.
