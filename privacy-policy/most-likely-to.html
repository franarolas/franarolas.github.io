Privacy Policy

Most Likely To

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Thank you for playing Most Likely To. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Most Likely To is a casual party game. We do not ask you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, or any other personally identifiable information. The game content runs entirely on your device.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The app uses a small number of third-party services that may collect limited, anonymized information to keep the app working and free to play:

  • Google AdMob — serves interstitial advertisements inside the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as your advertising ID), general device information (model, operating system, language), and broad location (country/region) to serve relevant ads and prevent fraud. You can control personalized advertising in your device settings.
  • Mixpanel — collects anonymous usage analytics (which screens are opened, how often the game is launched, which categories are selected). This data helps us understand how the app is used and improve it over time. It is not tied to your personal identity.

For details on how these services handle data, please review their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy (covers AdMob)
  • Mixpanel Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Most Likely To is intended for players aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

Your Choices

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at franarolas@gmail.com.
